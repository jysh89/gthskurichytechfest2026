About Tech Fest Extravaganza 2026

Tech Fest Extravaganza 2026 is the flagship annual technical festival of Government Technical High School Kurichy. The event celebrates innovation and practical learning by bringing together students to compete and showcase their talents in Electronics, Electrical Engineering, Information Technology, Science & Technology, Mathematics, Carpentry, Sheet Metal Work, and other specialized technical disciplines.

The festival aims to nurture creativity, teamwork and engineering excellence through hands-on competitions and interactive learning experiences.
